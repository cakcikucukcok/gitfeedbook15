My Testimony

As I walked inside the little church house

my mind drifted back to a day

when I was a lost sinner

and didn't know how to pray.

The church then begin singing

of how Jesus wanted to save

and on the old cross of Calvery

to us his life he gave.

Then in the back I heard shouting

prasies to Jesus on high

I know in my heart that moment

Hell was my home if I died.

Then something inside of me happened

the spirit of Jesus took hold

I knew the Altar was waiting

and to it I must go.

At the Altar I knelt on my knees and to the Lord I did pray then a small voice from Heaven said, get up my child you're saved.

Today I'm so thankful that the Lord cared enough for me he gave me a home in Heaven he saved me and set me free..
