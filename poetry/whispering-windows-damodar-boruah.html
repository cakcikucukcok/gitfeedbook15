Who can share life and death situation better than Damodar Boruah?'Living long is a success too',hardly one feels until a man,with all wisdom and wealth,thinks he will perish immatured for cardiac arrest any time without anyone's belief. Patient can rate a doctor for behaviour,listening mode,correct diagnosis and may not be a good prescriber yet his posture like a Godly incarnation inspires one to believe that he can live long still.See what he found in the chamber of Dr.Debojit Sharma,Jorhat. Can anyone think whether his sister,aunty,daughter or wife come home safe?How a college girl is molested by passengers and driver of a passenger vehicle.Poet depicts the incident in five poems in addition to 'Woman' section. Pathetic Small Tea Growers(STGs) stand own without the support of government even price mechanism is not laid strongly for the lackadaisical attitude of respective department as a result manufacturers,middle men take advantages of the situation inspite they sustain million households.Poet ask the govt to take special measure for these ailing STGs. Generation-Gap makes life severe to both children and parents.How two generations are suffering for mere misunderstanding,yet their goal is same-development and bring good name to home ,unfortunately one generation is not trying to understand another generation's heart for not trying to change and update due to change of time.He demonstrates clearly to have patience in dealing the situation in'All changed not father'. Sainik School Goalpara,his alma mater,he treats it like his temple where dedicated teachers preach,he says old boys have much to do for the institution with their experience. Hundred poems of contemporary problem shows one to come out from all vulnerable situations including 'Emotional Filter'.What readers want more from this tiny anthology?
