Wise thoughts of spirit to create a foundation,

Staying with a spirit to the Lords relation,

For all with spirit, theres a master plan

Ken Barker

Ken Barker considers himself a messenger of the Lord. With divine assistance, Barker shares a compilation of poetry that sheds light on sin, provides hope that the world can become a better place, and helps the spiritually lost find Jesus Christ and accept him as their Savior.

Barkers poems explore a variety of Christian-themed topics that encourage others to embrace the power of the Lord, confess their sins, find a church, perform good deeds, and loyally follow the commandments. In his reflections, Barker shares lyrical interpretations of well-known biblical stories while gently reminding us of Gods presence in all of our lives through prayer, faith, and fear of evil.

Christian and Religious Poems shares inspirational verse that highlights the importance of living in Gods trust and inspires the world to come together in the name of the Father, Son, and Holy Ghost.
