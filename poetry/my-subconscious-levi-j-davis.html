Being raised by a family of very strict religious beliefs, I was

forced to live a childhood of imprisonment and boredom. I

had to listen to a preacher every week ramble on about the

war between God and Satan to wreak havoc on my soul.

At the age of four I had my first encounter with a demon. I

know the apparition that appeared before me was sent by the

Devil himself. Thus, leading me into temptation and poisoning

my thoughts. This threw me into a state of confusion which

in-turn started the battle between good and evil within me.

Thereafter I begun to rebel against all that was instilled in my

mind. To this day I still vividly remember what I experienced

that night.

At the age of fourteen I met a man named Jack Redenuis who

influenced me to transform the conflicting thoughts running

through my head into words. One afternoon I was relaxing

in my room, listening to the radio when I decided to take

his advice. I began to write. It was then I realized how right

he was. By putting feelings and thoughts on paper, ones

reconciliation becomes plain and simple.
