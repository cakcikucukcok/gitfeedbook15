Adrift in the heavens floats a mystical star Created for lovers who dream, Of a world thats warm, gentle, and free, Where the grass is always green. Anthony Bane We are all on a unique journey through life that is sometimes happy and other times challenging. In a debut collection of diverse verse, Anthony Bane shares poetry that reflects on all that life offers, including love, natures beauty, and inner peace. Banes poems explore the hopes, joys, sadness, and pain we have all experienced at one time or another. While encouraging others to find courage in the face of challenges and not be afraid to express feelings, Bane leads others down a poignant path that begins with lyrical verse about love, loss, and faithfulness, and then explores the kind of bittersweet love that survives despite separation. The Journey of Life, from My Heart to Yours shares a moving compilation of poetry that reminds all of us that we are here on earth to love.
