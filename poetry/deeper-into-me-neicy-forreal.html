Poetry is used to express emotions associated with the highs, the in betweens and lows of life. Words uniquely and precisely arranged to include people of all walks of life. You will find the author does not ascribe a gender or ethnicity to the majority of her poetry. Thereby giving you, the reader, control to create the scenes and the characters. Take a journey into the author's mind and see if you will be inspired to action. See if you will be moved to embrace the inner you that is also yearning to be heard. See if you will be inspired to make your dreams a reality. See if you will find yourself touched by the authors openness to share her inner thoughts with you. See if you will find yourself saying, "That's exactly how I feel!" Wrap yourself around poetry that will sometimes take you by surprise. The passions of this poet will leave you craving for more.
