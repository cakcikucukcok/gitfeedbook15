Petey is as normal as can be. Even so, there is something very unique about Petey, something his mother gave him. Can you guess what it is? It's his name-Petey Pots and Pans. But not everybody realizes how special his name is. As a matter of fact, some people think it's a very strange name. They tease Petey Pots and Pans about his name, and that makes him feel just awful. One day, when Petey Pots and Pans wakes up with a stomachache, his mother takes him to see Dr. Bill. But when Dr. Bill can't find anything wrong with Petey, his mother is sure she understands why Petey Pots and Pans doesn't want to go to school. She has to do something to make Petey feel better, and quickly. Otherwise he could miss out on a good deal of learning. Petey's mother knows that Petey Pots and Pans could grow up to be anything he chooses. But she has to find a way to inspire Petey to be his very best even when others are mean to him. What will she do?
