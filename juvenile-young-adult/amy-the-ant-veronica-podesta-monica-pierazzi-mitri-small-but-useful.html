EXCERPT

They look like soldiers, thousands of soldiers marching along. All of them identical, one following the other, keeping a fast pace, with their brown silhouettes and their antennae always on the alert. Yet, one of them stands out of the regiment. She has a huge head and strong jaws. It’s Amy the anthill’s guardian.
