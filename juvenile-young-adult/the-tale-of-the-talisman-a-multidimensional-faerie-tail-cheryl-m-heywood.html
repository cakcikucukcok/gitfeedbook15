At the close of the Beltane celebration, WhiteMoon gracefully leads the villagers in thanking the Four Directions. As villagers bow in reverence to WhiteMoon, August collapses onto the forest floor. The crystal talisman he is wearing is radiant as a beam of moonlight breaks through the forest understory and illuminates its glassy surface. Follow Augusts journey back to health and vitality and discover the reason for his collapse. His transformation is fueled by the healing power of crystals, herbs, alchemy and other holistic therapies and is an empowering wakeup call for the village. Be a witness to how August learns that freedom is an inner journey of the heart.

* Expand your awareness of crystal metaphysical properties as ancient, sacred knowledge of the crystal kingdom is revealed in StarGates intuitive readings which are intended for the highest good of the village.

* Explore why villagers choose to wear crystal talismans and how they gain trust in the power of talismans to bring balance and harmony to their beings.

* Learn how the village is blessed to be supported by the synchrony of a higher dimensional winged trio responsible for solutions to village dilemmas and individuals emotional and physical challenges.

* Discover how villagers hearts awaken and the global heart of the community gains consciousness and thrives.

Open your heart and mind to another realm of possibility. Journey to a village full of awe, wonder and the magic of crystals where unconditional love, empowerment and transformation prevail.
