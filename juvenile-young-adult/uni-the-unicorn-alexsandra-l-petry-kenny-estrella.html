I wrote this book while I was watching all my Grandchildren growing up. I looked at what was available for them to read, watch on TV and see at the Movies. My Heart went out hoping for quality, happy, and positive themes to nourish their minds. Seeing that there is more of a need for positive adventures I wanted to create something beautiful for their minds, so I hope you enjoy it. Somewhere far away, there is a Beautiful Wonderland where a Very Special Unicorn lives. His name is Uni and he wants to share his Magical Kingdom with you. So come along for the Adventure Uni the Unicorn: The Great Adventure A simple story with all the right magical touch awaits readers when they read through the pages of author Alexsandra L. Petrys Uni the Unicorn. The book follows the story of a special unicorn that befriended five children, and its journey as it brought them to its Magical Kingdom. While in there, the kids spent the time enjoying various rides, visiting beautiful and magical spots around the kingdom, eating to their hearts content and finally ending the day with a powerful bonding experience and hopes of many more wonderful adventures before going to bed. With its simple and easy to understand words that make up the conversation between children and Uni, it will make this story very easy to grasp for a youngsters mind. Complete with lush illustrations, this will further propel childrens imagination resulting to a worthy and enjoyable reading and learning. Full of relatable childrens adventure along with lessons that every child should learn, Petrys book is a worthy and one recommended read.
