Auntie Gayles Daycare and Foster Care presents a fun and exciting childrens books that displays learning tools for children of all ages. Come and join Auntie Gayle and her day care children on a fun and exciting adventure. In each of these books, Auntie Gayle takes her children on new and exciting trips; she likes to call them Making Memories That Count. Each book will be a part of a collection of booksa total of fifteen. The first five editions to the collection are the following: 1. Auntie Gayles Daycare Goes Fishing 2. Auntie Gayles Daycare Goes to the Zoo 3. Auntie Gayles Daycare & The Snail Family 4. Auntie Gayles Daycare Learns How to Swim 5. Auntie Gayles Daycare Goes Swimming This is just the beginning of what we here at Auntie Gayles call outstanding learning tools. These books will be for children of all ages and will include full-color illustrations. Each book will start out with Auntie Gayles rules and regulations. These are the rules and regulations the day care must obey when in the care of Auntie Gayles Day Care and hopefully at all times.
