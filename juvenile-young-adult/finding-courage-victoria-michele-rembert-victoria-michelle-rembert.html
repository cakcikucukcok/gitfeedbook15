About the book The book is really about teaching kids that if you have a dream whatever it may be to not give up. You need to fully dedicate yourself to being the best you that you can possibly be. Success is not measured in whether you win or lose. Success is measured in how you rise to the top and how you stay there. How do you measure success? Whether you want to be a singer, a doctor, a dentist or even a actress. If you put in the work and do it the right way you will value it that much more when you reach your goals. Never give up on your dreams and never give up on yourself. All the people that said you could not do it will squirm even more when you reach what they say to be the impossible. Just remember who you are and remember how you got there.
