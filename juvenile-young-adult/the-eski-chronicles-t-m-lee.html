When a puppy named Eski wakes up to hear her cage at the animal shelter rattling, she is first nervous and then excited. A family has come to adopt her!

Eski is excited to meet Dad, Mom, and most of all, Emma, the little girl who is going to take care of her. Shes very happy to be going to a new home; theres a warm bed, tasty food, and toys and people to play with. But when she meets Rex and Debo, the two cats who already live with the family, they hiss and are not glad to see her. The cats are afraid Eski will take Emmas attention away, so they decide to play some tricks and get Mom and Dad to take the puppy away. Will Rex and Debo ever learn to get along with their new sister?

In the first installment of this childrens series, a newly adopted puppy settles in to her forever home and gets to know her new family. This exciting new series follows Eskis journey from being a young, lovable pup to a wise, grown dog. Children and adults alike will appreciate the lessons learned by Eski as she embarks on new adventures with her family and friends.
