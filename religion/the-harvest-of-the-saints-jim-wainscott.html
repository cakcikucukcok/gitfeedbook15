The Harvest of the Saints is a sequel to The Eleventh-Hour Overcomers. Its intent is to continue the overcoming theme by exploring a little-known rapture theory that has been hidden in the shadows for much of the last century. Unlike most rapture scenarios that rely upon a limited framework of supporting Scripture, the harvest concept is based on a compelling body of biblical evidence that also provides insight into other related end-time events. Specific topics include:

The clearly defined Old Testament pattern of a traditional harvest and its application to an end-time harvest of the saints.

Why Jesus will come both as a thief in the night and visibly in the clouds as our conquering King

The importance of overcoming and the Lords conditional promise concerning the hour of trial which shall come upon the whole world, to test those who dwell on the earth (Rev. 3:10)

Why outer darkness and the lake of fire represent two separate areas of confinement established to serve two entirely different purposes Why many of the details of Gods meticulously-designed end-time plan have been hidden from our eyes

Please join me as we bring the harvest concept out of the shadows and into the light where we can test all things; [and] hold fast what is good (1 Thess. 5:21). Jim Wainscott
