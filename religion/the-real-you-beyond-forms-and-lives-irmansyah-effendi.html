Who/what is our true self or spirit

How to communicate with our true self

The three human consciousnesses: brain, soul and spirit (true self)

The real us: understanding our true self

Reincarnation

Psychic abilities

Our heart and using our heart to surrender to the Creator

Our Inner heart and letting our inner heart be the director

Whether seeking spiritual connection, authentic happiness, or a deeper understanding of yourself and others, The Real You: Beyond Forms and Lives delivers a deeply profound and universal reminder of how we can best live the life weve been given to realize our true purpose and fulfill our ultimate destiny.
