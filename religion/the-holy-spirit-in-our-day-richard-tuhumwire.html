The Holy Spirit in Our Day is a book about the person and work of the third person of the Trinity – the Holy Spirit. In an age choking on expanses of information catapulted through the vast array of media channels; it is ironical that the knowledge that makes the real difference and transforms lives, such as shared in this book, is not easy to come by.

This book will show you that the Holy Spirit is God, a member of the Trinity equal in standing with the Father and the Son. Beyond this, you will learn that he is a real person with an independent mind, will and emotions, a person each of us can and is called relate and commune with.

In seven chapters written in easy to read English, the author covers diverse subjects such as shedding light on who the Holy Spirit really is, the different biblical symbols used for the person of the Holy Spirit, ways or channels by which the Holy Spirit comes into people’s lives, the multi-faceted work of the Holy Spirit and the often controversial subject of (functioning in) the gifts of the Holy Spirit. A special chapter has been added on the place of suffering in the ministry of the Holy Spirit.

Read this book to get answers to some deep questions in your life (and of many other people’s, like you) concerning the Holy Spirit. Learn the importance of cultivating a deeper relationship with the person of the Holy Spirit. Discover the secrets of God’s eternal power that created the universe and still moves age old mountains. Learn and overcome the many misconceptions and false doctrines on the person and work of the Holy Spirit.

Meet the one person you need most in your life to shape your destiny – The Holy Spirit in Our Day.
