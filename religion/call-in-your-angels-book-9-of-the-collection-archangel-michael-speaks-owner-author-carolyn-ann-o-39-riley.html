More of Archangel Michael's beautiful Channeled Messages, Meditations and Exercises. Each of the Channeled Messages has a guided meditation allowing each reader to experience what the message was actually discussing. The more you work with the meditations the more insightful they become. The messages contain higher information, glimpses into the Celestial Realm, sometimes spiritual gifts, often times processes to use for example clearing the Chakra system. Some messages encourage rest, fun and play. Each journey is different and each brings more and more depth to the spiritual journey that one finds themselves walking through. Archangel Michael believes in active participation by the reader to lift them up higher and higher on their spiritual spiral of evolution. It is through remembering that one finds the road to self-actualization. This book also contains some information, answers and exercises about intuitive questions that readers have asked.
