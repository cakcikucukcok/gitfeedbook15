• In the Unites States alone, at least a couple million adults belong to cults at any one time; millions more have spent time in cults or watched loved ones succumb to coercive control. Yet few understand how cults work. Mating in Captivity addresses this deficit by enveloping readers in the sensory details of a keenly observed cult experience. • The Fellowship for Intentional Community’s online directory lists over 1,000 collective groups in the United States (plus another 400 in other countries) —and the FIC estimates that hundreds of thousands of people worldwide have either lived in or formed a significant attachment to an intentional community. Mating in Captivity illuminates a shadow side of the communitarian impulse. • In 2016, the number of Americans in default on federal student loans reached a record eight million.
