Jacqueline Carrington is a young, wealthy, intelligent woman who is determined to have it all. She has always been accustomed to having things her way at all times. At one point in her life she came to a fork in the road. Jacqueline wants to take the road to love everlasting and wedded bliss with the love of her life for the last past 2 and half years. Her future is looking very promising and bright until her past decides to ring her phone. Just when she had gotten everything back on track past hurts, past love, and past emotions are hot on her trail. Jacqueline comes to a place where only God can show her the way to go. Will Jacqueline receive her happily ever after or will her past rob her of the man she loves and their bright future?
