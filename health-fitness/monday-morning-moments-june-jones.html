In this unique work Monday Morning Moments, June Jones offers insights into the timeless messages for everyday living that the Bible presents. Writing early every Monday morning has been a way for her to reach out to others and share her Holy Spirit-inspired words of wisdom and encouragement. The author finds that writing early on Monday morning is a way to prioritize ones daily activities and free the mind from doubt and worry. Writing on a variety of topics, such as leadership, making choices, and godly living, the author uses scripture and gentle encouragement to nudge readers to a new understanding of both obstacles and blessings. She encourages her readers to take up writing themselves, whether to tell someone they are loved or missed, to remember the good and work on the bad, to take a stand against oppression, or to remember lifes important events.
