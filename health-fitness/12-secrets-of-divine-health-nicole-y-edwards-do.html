Spiritual leaders and parishioners are givers by definition, selflessly devoting all of themselves to their congregations and communities, often to the detriment of their personal health. In The 12 Secrets of Divine Health, physician and minister Dr. Nicole Edwards takes a detailed look at critical health practices that are often neglected, providing a comprehensive overview of physical, mental, spiritual, and financial habits that result in an enriched whole-man-health lifestyle. This insightful guidebook also provides worksheets and exercises to ensure the reader’s success in their journey to a healthier, more abundant life.
