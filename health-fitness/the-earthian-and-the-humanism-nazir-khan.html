GENERALLY THIS BOOK IS WRITTEN WITH THE THOUGHTS OF A REFORMER AND A THINKER IN THE SERVICE OF MAN KIND FOR THE SAKE OF UNITY, HUMANITY, PEACE AND PROSPERITY AND JOY FULL LIVING OF ALL THE CITIZENS OF THIS BEAUTIFUL WORLD,. ULTIMATELY, THIS IS A LITERATURE AND FICTION BOOK IN THE FORM OF A SPICY NOVEL.
