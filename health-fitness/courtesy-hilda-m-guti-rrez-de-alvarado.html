Title: COURTESY!

Subtitle: Good Manners!

This book is dedicated to all children who, at home and outside their home, practice their Good Manners and give themselves and others Respect and Courtesy.

Education begins at home, not in school. The good things (VALUES) you learned at home, such as good manners and respect (to yourself and others)are reinforced at school; However, they begin at home.
