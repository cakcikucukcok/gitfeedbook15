"This Crown was Made for You: The Queen You Were Destined to Be" will encourage married women and hopefuls that they can be the queens of their castles when they do things God's way. All that means is loving God and honoring the man He gave them as a guide throughout their married lives - their husbands! Author Danielle Wilson Rosser wrote this book after her own experiences taught her that things work out better when she stuck to God's plan. She learned that respecting her husband and showing genuine appreciation for his efforts led to an even greater outpouring of love and support from him. If you're wondering why your man doesn't do more for you, or he acts out in ways that frustrate or embarrass you, you need to read this book! If you're single and desire a healthy, happy marriage one day, this book will show you how to be the wife who brings out the best in her man and enjoys the title of "Queen!"
