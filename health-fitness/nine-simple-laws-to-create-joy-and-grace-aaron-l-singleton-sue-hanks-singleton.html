Sue Hanks Singletons near-death experience provided her with an invaluable vision about the big picture and how the universe works. In Nine Simple Laws to Create Joy and Grace, she shares a treasure trove of wisdom on reclaiming and using the spiritual underpinnings of the universal laws to create a more conscious, fulfilled, and dynamic life.

Sue and her husband and coauthor, Aaron L. Singleton, reveal which universal laws are the architects with blueprints; which laws are the reliable builders and carpenters who carry out the architectural plans; and which laws provide the infrastructure, much like electricians and plumbers, to build your path to happiness. With a chapter dedicated to each law, this guide includes descriptions, examples, quotations, and artwork. This is followed by exercises to enhance your understanding of the law and to practice it on your own, a daily affirmation for each law to help you focus, and Stone Play Crystal Layouts for each law.

Intentionally written with many layers of understanding, Nine Simple Laws to Create Joy and Grace helps you discover the timeless and joyful wisdom of living in cooperation with the heightened awareness of the nine universal laws. Learn to begin anew and to manifest joy and grace in your life.
