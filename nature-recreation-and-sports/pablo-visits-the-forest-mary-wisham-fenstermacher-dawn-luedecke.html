Pablo Visits the Forest is the third book in the Pablo Adventure Series. Pablo is a warm weather (African) penguin that loves adventure. Guided by the park ranger, Pablo and Carmelita learn about the ancient trees of Americas northwest. Even within the beautiful surroundings, the visitors learn that danger can lurk in the shadows of the Redwood Forest. Come join Pablo and his friends as they explore the secrets under the forest canopy!
