Australian Horse Behaviour Specialist. Creator of the 'NO Buck!' Horse Starting Course, Self-Medicate™ and the best selling home study program; Your Key to Success with Horses. Steve Brinkworth is an internationally recognized horse trainer, rider and equine consultant who has helped motivate and inspire horse owners all over the world to solve almost every conceivable horse-related problem. Visit his website: www.SteveBrinkworth.com

Steve Brinkworth's horsemanship journey started in 1979 when his parents bought Charlie, a small horse of unknown origins. From there, the horse 'bug' set in and the Brinkworth family were hooked. Together they created 'Flinders Rides,' a successful long-distance horse trekking business.

From a young age, Steve was guiding international clientele into the rugged Flinders Ranges, for weeklong horseback excursions through the magnificent Australian Outback.

Steve became very successful in the sport of 'Endurance Riding' and completed thousands of miles on his horses, including a couple of the now famous, 'Tom Quilty' endurance rides. Each time Steve was presented with the highly respected 'Quilty Buckle'.

With his Arabian Stallion 'Paladdin Sayyid' Steve won numerous awards and trophies, including the coveted 'High Point, Pure Arabian, Endurance Horse of the Year'.

Through decades of teaching both humans and horses, Steve has developed a practical, concise and straightforward approach to horse training. By following Steve's Horsemanship Essentials Program, students regularly report achieving great results and enjoying a wonderful relationship with their horse.

Steve lives in Australia with his wife Sally, where they run their business; Horsemanship Essentials.
