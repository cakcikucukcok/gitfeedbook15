Bobbi Stratos PI is everyone’s go-to girl now facing burnout. What she needs is a holiday. What she gets is to be thrown blind into a deadly movieland multiple homicide case with a bully film producer and all his baggage, an individualistic Boston cop obsessed with a little girl’s murder, a sadistic hit man whose specialty is knife work, and a body count that just keeps rising. Trouble begins when Lou Lazar, neurotic Hollywood studio boss at Bugatti Films, sends Bobbi to Boston at big bucks to sort out a growing mess there when one of his line producers shows up dead. Bobbi walks off the plane and straight into Connell's life. Can they work together in time to bring down a secretive crime group and their sadistic blade-loving killer? Det. Ty Connell is a hybrid character based on police officers Michael Hughes has known personally, at the city, state, and federal levels. He also counts as an invaluable research source a formerly active member of one of New York City's major crime families, now retired.
