CONFESSION OF SIN – Not All Secrets Should Be Kept – is a police procedure - detective - crime thriller full of disturbing twists.

It is late November in Chicago and blood coats the steps at St. Florentine Cathedral. Lieutenant Damien Kaine, from the Vicious Crimes Unit, and his partner Detective Joe Hagan stand over the mutilated body of Father Mandahari. 

A note left by the killer indicates the Catholic Church is hiding secrets, and those secrets are getting priests killed. 

After a second priest is violently murdered, Damien and Joe find themselves in a battle with the Catholic Church to get information that will help them narrow in on a suspect. 

Not only is Damien dealing with a ruthless killer, he has a secret admirer who wants his attention. When Damien receives a gift, it becomes apparent this is no schoolgirl crush.

Damien will stop at nothing to protect those he loves, and it is that desperation that puts he and Joe in a situation with deadly consequences. 

This book mirrors a painstaking police murder investigation. 

If you are a fan of CSI, CRIMINAL MINDS, and MOTIVE, then this new crime fiction series is for you.
