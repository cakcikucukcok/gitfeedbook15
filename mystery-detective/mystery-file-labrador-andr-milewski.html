Oslo, 1952:

Young German, Max Falkenburg, works as a museum guard in the Viking Ship Museum. When he prevents the theft of a mysterious medallion, he unexpectedly finds himself on the hunt for Mjölnir, Thor’s legendary hammer.

Together with American archaeologist Frederick Crichton and his students, Max throws himself into finding the mythological artefact. But a secret division of the Russian Secret Service is hot on their heels, and will stop at nothing to get their hands on the hammer.

The search leads Max and his friends from Iceland to Greenland, and finally to the coast of Labrador, where the stage is set for the final battle for Mjölnir – a battle which will decide all their fates…
