Graded Reader (Intermediate) - Evan O'Gorman is a young Irish rower who is coached by his father and is trying to qualify for the Olympics. One morning while rowing, Evan's oar hits a dead horse in the River Liffey. It starts a series of events that leads him to Spain to search for a girl there... but it also leads him into great danger. This book is specifically written to provide easy and enjoyable reading for English language learners. It is an adaptation of an original contemporary novel. This book is most suitable for language students of B1 level upwards, particularly B2 (Upper Intermediate) to C1 (Advanced). It includes an Introduction to the story, Illustrations, a Glossary of difficult words and Activities.
