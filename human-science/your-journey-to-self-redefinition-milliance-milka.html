The Heroine’s Journey to Self-Redefinition is an invitation to explore how women can develop their self-identity consciously by journeying within to identify their source of power. It is a personal development book that uses storytelling and reflective activities to help identify personal values and motives. It provides actionable steps to create alignment between one's thoughts, beliefs and one's actions to minimize fear of the future and regret of the past - suffering. Self-belief is the beginning of the journey to courageously reset. 

Crucial themes covered in this book are journeys, transitions, and transformations, which are crucial for women to develop  their whole self to balance their feminine and masculine energies. This is an excellent book for women undergoing a period of change in their lives from moving to a new country; becoming a mother; taking on a leadership role; starting a second professional act later in life or entering into retirement.
