Introduction: EXPERIENCING EMOTIONS

Chapter 1: WHAT ARE THE EMOTIONS WHAT ARE THEY FOR?: The Coordination of Emotional Processes Structures, the Mental Universe operating system, the operating system of Emotional Processes, Development of the concept of good and evil;

Chapter 2: EDUCATE TO LIVE THE EMOTIONS

Chapter 3: WHY EDUCATE TO LIVE THE EMOTIONS: The function of Emotions in the Company, the function of psychology in the Company, the Psychological Sciences, Human Sciences, Psychology of Wellness, Culture Welfare Custom.

Chapter 5: SPIRITUALITY HUMAN

The realization of the PSYCHIC UNIVERSE: EDUCATING THE EMOTIONS model allows each individual to express their mental world and to reflect on itself, at the same time allows you to understand others and to evolve specific personality characteristics within a social context welcoming and stimulating.

This book wants to offer a dynamic model to undertake the psychic path of KNOWING ONESELF, both individually, and in groups and / or social context.

Educate yourself to live the emotions is as important as learning science and computer techniques and knowledge to intervene in reality, so as to become capable people to take action and to decide independently and creatively in democratic societies.
