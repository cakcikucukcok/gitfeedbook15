So, we are here.

What happens once we die?

Is there an afterlife? Is there eternity? Where is it? How is it?

Do we really live for ever?

Somehow, deep down, we know there is more and wonder what will happen to us after leaving this world.

Stories from the Other Side is based on Annys numerous encounters with the dead that have occurred during hypnotherapy sessions. Based on the information Anny has collected from these spirits, she decided to shed light on what happens after we die. By making them talk about themselves, Anny learned the experience is not about bright lights, heaven, hell and the like.

Transcripts of conversations with spirits do reveal what happens next.

Join Anny as she shares fascinating real life stories from the ones who passed to the next plane.
